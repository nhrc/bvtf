INTERVIEWS

Rev. Dr. Mike Van Buren - Fellowship Reformed of Hudsonville, MI

Presented his view on:

  • The church's foundation must be the Word of God.
  • When it comes to church structure, ask questions that no one has asked before. If questions were once dismissed, ask them again.
  • Define your direction (Staff comes up with their dreams, consistory makes the decisions, teams give direction).
  • The church grows in proportion to the organizational structure that is put into place.
  • Attendance drove the building projects.

Rev. Jonathan Elgersma - Faith Reformed Church of Zeeland, MI

Presented his view on:

  • When you set your budget, account for costs of furnishings, heating/cooling/maintaining building, potential additional staff members, etc.
  • Build programs around the people of the church, NOT the pastor. Church needs to be able to maintain programs even if pastor leaves.
