OFF-SITE VISITS

Faith Reformed Church

We chose Faith Reformed Church to learn about challenges and ideas to consider during a building process.

In the early 2000's Faith Reformed Church undertook a major building expansion, adding a multipurpose room, youth rooms, kitchen, and multiple classrooms.

Following are some of the main ideas:

  • Key card entry for security - logs people coming/going from the building, easy to set for various volunteers.
  • Clear and understandable signs.
  • Coffee area with large open space for fellowship.
  • New elevators with easy accessibility.
  • Wheelchair-accessible bathrooms on same level as entrance.
  • Multiple lighting options in gym (gym lights vs. lights for worship services).
  • Large youth room - requires sound system.
  • Thumb print scanner to print name tags.
  • Numerous seating areas.
  • Larger mailboxes for members (large enough to fit small boxes and packages) - some near the floor and hard to access.
