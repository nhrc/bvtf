PREFACE

Introduction/Purpose of the Building Vision Task Force (BVTF)

  • In the spring of 2012, the Consistory put a team of 10 church members together to make up a team called the Building Vision Task Force. This team’s mission was to investigate the facility needs of North Holland Reformed Church (NHRC) and to present a report that makes recommendations and identifies priorities relating to building changes/improvements/projects. The results are summarized in this presentation.
  • To accomplish this task the BVTF conducted interviews, visited off-site facilities and distributed questionnaires to the NHRC congregation, staff, and ministry teams.
  • NHRC’s Vision: Intentionally sharing the love of Jesus through compassion-filled hospitality. This vision has been the guiding principal throughout this process.
